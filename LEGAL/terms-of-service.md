## 2. What ScholarKeep does — and the limit of what it can promise

ScholarKeep helps you:

- Capture and organize receipts on your phone
- Estimate whether a purchase appears eligible under your scholarship program
- Track the documentation checklist that the official portal will likely require
- Walk a reimbursement claim through a parent-maintained lifecycle (Draft → Submitted → Approved → Paid)
- Generate submission-package PDFs and CSV exports (Pro feature)
- Surface deadline reminders as local notifications

**Estimates only.** The eligibility verdicts ScholarKeep shows are calculated from a versioned JSON ruleset that mirrors the most recent FES-UA, FES-EO, and PEP Purchasing Guides we are aware of. Florida's rules change every school year. We do our best to keep the ruleset current, but the **official Purchasing Guide and Family Handbook for your scholarship is always the controlling source**, and you must verify your purchase against it before relying on a reimbursement.

**No guarantee.** We do not, and cannot, guarantee that any purchase you make based on a ScholarKeep estimate will be reimbursed by your Scholarship Funding Organization. Your SFO reviews every claim independently and may approve, deny, or hold a claim for reasons outside ScholarKeep's knowledge.
