Privacy Policy

Effective: May 23, 2026 · Last updated: May 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data the ScholarKeep mobile application ("ScholarKeep", "the app", "we", "us") collects, stores, and shares. ScholarKeep is operated by Carlos Reyes as an independent iOS application available through the Apple App Store.

Plain English. Everything you put in ScholarKeep stays on your phone. We do not have servers that hold your data. We do not sell, share, rent, or transmit your records to anyone. Sign in with Apple is used only to lock the app to your Apple ID; the Apple identifier never leaves your device. If you turn on iCloud backup, the data syncs through your own iCloud account.

1. What we do NOT collect

ScholarKeep does not collect or transmit usage analytics, does not include third-party SDKs that collect data, does not track you across other apps, does not send your records to any server we operate (we don't operate any), does not sell or share information, and does not use your data for advertising.

2. What ScholarKeep stores locally on your device

Student profiles, expenses, receipts, line items, attachments, providers, pre-authorizations, refunds, balance ledger entries, claims, status history. All stored exclusively in the app's private container.

3. Sign in with Apple

Used to gate access. Apple shares a stable identifier (unique to ScholarKeep + your Apple ID) which is stored locally. The identifier never leaves your device. Apple's own privacy practices apply to the sign-in flow itself.

4. Subscriptions (StoreKit 2)

Pro is delivered as an in-app subscription processed by Apple. Apple handles payment, taxation, and billing. The app checks subscription entitlement on-device. Apple collects payment info under Apple's terms; we do not.

5. On-device OCR (Apple Vision)

When you scan a receipt, Apple Vision extracts text on the device itself. The receipt image is not transmitted off the device.

6. Notifications

Local notifications only — scheduled by iOS on your device for deadlines and on-hold clocks. No remote push.

7. Optional iCloud backup (off by default)

If you enable iCloud backup in Settings, your records sync through your personal iCloud account using Apple CloudKit. Encrypted in transit by Apple, stored under your Apple ID. Disabling the toggle stops further sync.

8. Diagnostics (MetricKit)

iOS may share aggregated and anonymized performance metrics via Apple's MetricKit framework. These payloads do not contain your records and are written to a local diagnostics folder. We do not transmit them.

9. Children's privacy

ScholarKeep is designed for parents and guardians. Not intended for use by children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Your rights

  • Access and export: Settings → Export all expenses (CSV).
  • Deletion: Settings → Delete all data on this device.
  • Sign-out: Settings → Account → Sign out.
  • App removal: Deleting the app from iOS removes all locally-stored data.

11. Third-party services we do not use

ScholarKeep does NOT integrate with Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, Facebook, TikTok, Sentry, Bugsnag, Crashlytics, or any analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting service. ScholarKeep does NOT interact with Step Up For Students, AAA Scholarship Foundation, EMA, SMP, MyScholarShop, Tipalti, FLDOE, or any state of Florida system.

12. Changes

We may update this policy when we change how the app handles data. Material changes will be highlighted in the next app release notes.
