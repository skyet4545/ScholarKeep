Common questions

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?
iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → ScholarKeep → Cancel.

How do I request a refund?
Refunds are processed by Apple, not by us. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com within 60 days of the charge.

I think a verdict is wrong. What do I do?
The eligibility ruleset is bundled with the app and traceable to the Purchasing Guide section. If you find a discrepancy, email us with the receipt details and the section of the guide you think contradicts it — we'll update the ruleset and push it to all users.

Will my data sync to my iPad?
Optional iCloud backup is a Pro feature. Until you enable it, data stays only on the device where you entered it.

How do I export everything?
Pro: Settings → Export all expenses (CSV). Or Reports tab → Export filtered (CSV) for a subset.

How do I delete everything?
Settings → Delete all data on this device. Requires double-confirmation. Cannot be undone.
