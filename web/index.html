FAQ

Is ScholarKeep affiliated with Step Up For Students or the state of Florida?

No. ScholarKeep is an independent personal-records tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Florida Department of Education, Step Up For Students, AAA Scholarship Foundation, EMA, SMP, or any official scholarship system. You submit your claims through the official portal yourself — ScholarKeep helps you organize and verify.

Are eligibility verdicts guaranteed?

No. Every verdict is an estimate based on the published 2025-26 Purchasing Guides. Your Scholarship Funding Organization reviews every claim independently. ScholarKeep helps you avoid the most common denial reasons; it can't guarantee approval.

Does my data ever leave my phone?

Only if you turn on iCloud backup (Pro), in which case it syncs through your personal Apple iCloud — not through us. We don't operate any servers.

Which programs do you support?

All three Florida ESA programs: FES-UA, FES-EO, and PEP.

How do I cancel a Pro subscription?

iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

Will my receipts auto-import from email?

Not in this release. Email forwarding would require a backend we don't run. If demand justifies it, we'll add it as a Family-tier feature in a future release.
